🏛️ Government & Law Enforcement Requests

BurgTV responds to valid legal process from law enforcement agencies. However, due to the nature of our service (a media player, not a streaming provider), we typically have very limited user data to share.

What data BurgTV can provide

  • Account email (if user has a Premium account)
  • Subscription transaction IDs (Stripe)
  • Last sign-in IP address (for Premium users)

What data BurgTV does NOT have

  • Content watched by users (we do not log playback)
  • User M3U / Xtream credentials (stored only on device, encrypted)
  • Streaming source URLs (handled device-side)
  • Personal viewing history (stored only on user device)
